Brian Styles - The St. Louis Balloon Guy

With his goofy antics and witty one-liners, Brian Styles has been entertaining audiences for over fifteen years in St. Louis. From creating one-of-a-kind elaborate balloon art, to baffling groups with mystifying comedy magic, Brian is sure to be a hit at any event. Throughout the years, Brian has entertained at birthday parties, school functions, weddings, grand openings, restaurants, and the list goes on and on. With his belief in creating a personable relationship with each and every client, it's no wonder people have Brian back year after year.
