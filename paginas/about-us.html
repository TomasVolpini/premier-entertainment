About us

Meet Brian Styles,

the St. Louis Balloon Guy

With his goofy antics and witty one-liners, Brian Styles has been entertaining audiences for over fifteen years in St. Louis. Throughout the years, Brian has entertained at birthday parties, school functions, weddings, grand openings, restaurants, and the list goes on and on. From creating one-of-a-kind elaborate balloon art, to baffling groups with mystifying comedy magic, Brian is sure to be a hit at any event. Come join the fun, bring a suggestion, and watch the magic happen in St. Louis.

Brian Styles is the fastest, most elaborate, interactive, personable balloon artist in the St. Louis area.

A star was born

Premier Entertainment it is!

What started out as a one man balloon company, Premier Entertainment has quickly grown to include St. Louis' best balloon artists, face painters, magicians, and caricature artists. Our company philosophy is simple; To be the best at what we do. From the initial consultation, to the moment we are packing up from your event, we want it to be perfect! Our entertainers work as a team and are constantly brainstorming how we can do better.
